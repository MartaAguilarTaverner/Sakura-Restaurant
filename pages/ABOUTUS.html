Sakura Restaurant and Coffee

In Sakura Restaurant and coffee we want to give to our clients the most autenthic and incredible experience.
We wanted to transform our local in a way that our clients will be able to feel like they are in Japan inside our walls. With a big selection of traditional dishes, deserts and drinks of Japan, completely handmade and with the best products, we want to bring closer the importancy of the fresh products that are attached to nippon culture.
If you want to be able to get to know the culture and japanese tradition, we invite you to travel around it with our dishes.
